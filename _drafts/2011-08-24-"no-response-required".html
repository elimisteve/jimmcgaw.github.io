--- layout: post title: '"No Response Required"' date: '2011-08-24T15:53:00.001-07:00' author: Jim McGaw tags: modified_time: '2011-08-24T15:58:29.681-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-8627213696846591587.post-8839961496040036914 --- The three sweetest letters you can tack on to the end of any email you send to a person: NRR. If you think they might not understand the acronym, then spell it out.

I send several emails a day, and many of them don't require a response. I'd like to think that any person who has ever sent me an email has gotten a reply in a timely fashion. My goal is to answer all of them, when needed.

But I send a lot of email inquiries that don't require a response. Sometimes a response would be nice, but I don't want to obligate the person to reply. It takes time, effort, and care in figuring out what to write to someone who has just written you.
