--- layout: post title: The First and the Follow-Up date: '2011-07-17T13:39:00.001-07:00' author: Jim McGaw tags: modified_time: '2011-07-17T13:46:34.604-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-8627213696846591587.post-7739896164004137975 --- I don't actually think that the first thing you do is all that difficult.

The first book, the first movie, the first thing you ever do is not bogged down by anyone's expectations.

Ivan Reitman could have just stopped after he did Animal House, or maybe after he got around to directing Stripes. Once you hit the nail on the head, you feel like the only thing you can possibly do is screw up.
