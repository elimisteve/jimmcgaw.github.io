--- layout: post title: The People Who Change the World... --- ...are the ones who are insane enough to think that they can. There will always be people who will say to you, "Don't do that." This is how becoming a little hard of hearing can be a major virtue.

Someone once asked Helen Keller if she thought there was anything worse than being blind. She replied, "Yes, there is something worse...it would be to be able to see, but to not have any vision."

In the land of the blind, the man with one eye is king. Almost all of us two eyes, but there's an awful lot of things we don't see because we're busy not looking for them.
