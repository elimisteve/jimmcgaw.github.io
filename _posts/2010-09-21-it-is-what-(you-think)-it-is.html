--- layout: post title: It Is What (You Think) It Is --- The world around us is not simply the way it is; it's the way we think it is. Reality is perception. It's the way we interpret it in our brains. Things are the way we think they are.

Not a new concept, or even that insightful. But to this, it's worth adding: things have the potential to be what you think they can be. That's your opportunity.
