During the past ten years of my life, I've actually been on both sides of this particular global warming/climate change debate. This doesn't mean that I'm an open-minded person. This simply means that I have, in the past, been prone to accepting different kinds of rhetoric put forth by different groups, which isn't necessarily something I would bill as a strength.

This started in college, when I became aware of environmental issues, and that I was on the verge of entering the adult world for the first time. As an adult, I figured it would be sensible for me to at least attempt to make responsible decisions. And since I had grown up hearing, over and over, that our environment was on the verge of collapse, I approached the situation from that vantage point.

So during college, I accepted the mindset that businesses, while they do a lot of good, don't always operate with environmental concerns as their highest priority. Not all businesses, but the larger ones tend to impact everything more heavily, like the economy and our legislation, so it made more sense to focus on them.
